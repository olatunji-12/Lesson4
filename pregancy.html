Pregnancy at 12 Weeks

Physical Changes:

  • Reduced Symptoms: Nausea and fatigue may decrease.
  • Increased Energy: Many women feel more energetic.
  • Noticeable Bump: A small baby bump may be visible.
  • Weight Gain: Normal to gain around 1-2 kg (2-4 lbs).

Baby Development:

  • Growth Spurts: Baby is about the size of a lime.
  • Organ Development: Major organs forming and functioning.
  • Heartbeat: Can be heard with a Doppler device.

Health and Care:

  • Continue Prenatal Vitamins: Support baby's development.
  • Healthy Diet: Eat a balanced diet rich in nutrients.
  • Regular Check-ups: Monitor baby's progress and your health.

Emotional Changes:

  • Mood Swings: Hormonal changes can cause mood swings.
  • Excitement and Anxiety: Common feelings about the pregnancy.

Next Steps:

  • Screening Tests: Possible genetic condition screening.
  • Share the News: Many choose to announce pregnancy now.
